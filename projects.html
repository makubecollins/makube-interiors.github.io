Our Completed Projects

Urban Chic Apartment Redesign

This project involved a complete redesign of a small urban apartment, transforming it into a stylish and functional living space. Key elements included custom built-in shelving, a minimalist color palette, and strategically placed accent lighting. The client wanted a modern, yet comfortable atmosphere, and we achieved this by combining sleek furniture with warm, textured fabrics.

Contemporary Office Space Transformation

This project involved the complete overhaul of a dated office space into a modern, collaborative environment. We focused on creating open workspaces, integrating ergonomic furniture, and using natural light to enhance productivity. The design incorporated a bold color scheme and modular elements to create a dynamic and inspiring workspace.

Mid-Century Modern Living Room Revival

This project aimed to revive the classic mid-century modern style in a client's living room. We carefully selected vintage furniture pieces, incorporated warm wood tones, and used geometric patterns to create a nostalgic yet contemporary space. The focus was on creating a comfortable and stylish area for both relaxation and entertaining.

Luxury Spa Bathroom Redesign

This project transformed a standard bathroom into a luxurious spa-like retreat. We incorporated natural stone, a freestanding soaking tub, and a custom-designed steam shower. The design focused on creating a calming and serene environment with soft lighting and high-end fixtures.

Custom Entertainment Center Design

This project focused on designing a bespoke entertainment center that seamlessly integrated technology with elegant design. We incorporated hidden cable management, custom shelving for media components, and ambient lighting to create a cinematic experience within the client's living room.

Custom Staircase and Foyer Design

Project description 6: This project involved a complete redesign of the home's entryway, featuring a custom-designed staircase that served as a striking focal point. We incorporated elegant materials, dramatic lighting, and bespoke railings to create a grand and welcoming entrance.
